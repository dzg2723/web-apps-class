Poppyseed Chicken

This is a soupy chicken dish to be eaten over rice. Contains dairy, wheat, and meat.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Cream of Chicken Soup
  • Sour Cream
  • Poppyseeds

Instructions

  1. Cook the Chicken and tear it up into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Mix cream of chicken soup, sour cream, and poppyseeds together.
  3. Pour half of mixture in a baking dish, add chicken on top, and pour remaining mixture over chicken.
  4. Bake dish for 30 minutes at 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
